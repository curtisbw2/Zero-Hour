Josh

Investor / Interviewer

Mississippi

Josh Frazier is a former educator who became a self-taught software developer out of frustration with the limitations of existing K-12 technology. This led him to launch his own company more than ten years ago, where he focuses on building and managing software systems that handle K-12 student data at scale.

Beyond software, Josh builds homes and is actively involved in investing communities. These parallel paths have reinforced a systems-driven approach to problem solving and long-term value creation.

While these pursuits have defined much of his professional journey, Josh is currently in the early planning stages of developing a technology-driven system aimed at preventing child drownings.
