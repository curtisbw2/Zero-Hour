Meet the Team

Three investors dedicated to discovering and interviewing emerging companies in the defense and tech sector.

Investor / Interviewer

JT is the owner of Hidden Gems Research LLC - a verified Substack Bestseller followed by thousands of investors wanting to learn more about the emerging drone, energy, and autonomous robotics sectors. He has a background in finance with nearly a decade of experience in variable indexed annuities. Benny

Investor / Interviewer

He enjoys the outdoors and investing, particularly in small to mid-cap defense, drone and drone-adjacent companies. Benny has been known to travel coast to coast in search of the best organizations and leaders in which to invest.
