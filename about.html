ABOUT ZHG

Our Mission

FINDING SIGNALS
IN THE NOISE

Zero Hour Group is a team of seasoned retail investors specializing in the defense and technology sector, with a focus on drones, autonomous systems, counter-drone (C-UAS) technology, ISR, and related emerging defense tech.

Our core mission is to discover undervalued equities and conduct in-depth interviews with emerging market leaders, surfacing the signals that actually matter: customer traction, competitive positioning, financial discipline, and near-term catalysts.

We are not a hedge fund, newsletter service, or formal advisory. We are content creators and analysts who build community around small-cap and micro-cap defense and tech plays through high-quality interviews, earnings coverage, and collaborative research.

How We Work

METHODOLOGY

PRIMARY SOURCE ACCESS

We go directly to CEOs, CFOs, and founders, not analyst reports or secondhand summaries. Long-form interviews surface nuance that headlines miss.

EXECUTION OVER HYPE

We track quarterly execution, backlog growth, revenue trajectory, partnership depth, and technology milestones. Price action alone tells us nothing.

SECTOR EXPERTISE

Defense and technology is a specialized space. We speak the language, C-UAS, ISR, BVR, UAS, TAM, and we hold companies accountable to it.

COMMUNITY TRANSPARENCY

We share our research openly, collaborate with other retail investors, and credit our sources. The edge is community, not secrecy.
