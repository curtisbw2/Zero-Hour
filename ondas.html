$ONDS Ondas Holdings, Inc. Drones · C-UAS · Autonomous Systems

Ondas Holdings is a drone and autonomous systems company building hardware, software, and services for national security and industrial applications. Current focus areas include counter-drone (C-UAS) technology, the Optimus autonomous drone-in-a-box platform, and a growing defense backlog backed by a $1B+ institutional capital raise. Nasdaq-listed.
