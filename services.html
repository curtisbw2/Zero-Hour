WHAT WE DO

We help emerging defense and tech companies get in front of retail investors — conducting structured interviews with leadership teams and publishing the intelligence investors need to make informed decisions.

COMPANY INTERVIEWS

We conduct direct, on-record interviews with key company executives, including:

  • Board of Directors
  • Chief Executive Officers (CEO)
  • Chief Financial Officers (CFO)

Our interviews are structured to surface the information that matters most: customer traction, competitive positioning, capital discipline, and near-term catalysts.

RESEARCH & REPORTS

Interview findings are published as clear, investor-friendly written summaries and highlights through Hidden Gems Research LLC — a verified Substack Bestseller. We translate complex company stories into actionable intelligence for serious retail investors.

INVESTOR DISCOVERY

We actively source investment opportunities by traveling coast to coast — identifying small to mid-cap defense, drone, and tech plays before they reach mainstream coverage. Our network and on-the-ground research give subscribers an edge.
