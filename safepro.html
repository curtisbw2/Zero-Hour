Nasdaq: $SPAI  ·  AI · Explosive Threat Detection · Drones

$SPAI Safe Pro Group, Inc. AI · Explosive Threat Detection · Drones

Mission-driven technology company delivering AI-enabled security and defense solutions. Specializes in drone imagery processing using commercially available off-the-shelf drones combined with proprietary machine learning and computer vision to enable rapid identification of explosive threats — a safer, more scalable alternative to traditional human-based analysis.

Flagship platform SpotlightAI detects and labels over 150 types of explosive threats including landmines, cluster munitions, and unexploded ordnance (UXO). The platform has processed over 1.88 million battlefield images and identified 34,200 threats — building the world's largest datasets of real-world mines and UXO from operations in Ukraine. Selected by U.S. Army Futures Command for CFWE-M 2026 and integrated into the U.S. Army's ATAK platform for real-time threat sharing across military devices.

Subsidiaries: Airborne Response (autonomous drone services), Safe-Pro USA (ballistic protective gear).
