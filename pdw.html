Private Performance Drone Works Tactical UAS · Defense Manufacturing · Autonomous Systems

Manufacturer of advanced robotic systems for front-line forces, founded by combat veterans and drone engineers. Platforms include advanced unmanned aircraft, mission software, training platforms, and resilient communications and navigation systems. The C100 Tactical UAS is a modular, AI-enabled attritable quadcopter designed for front-line military operations, supported by a full-stack product suite including a Range Extension Kit, anti-jam radio, and mission planning software.

Raised more than $110 million in Series B funding in March 2026. Drone Factory 01 spans 90,000 sq. ft. in Huntsville, Alabama. Notable contracts include a $20 million U.S. Army contract tied to the Transformation in Contact program and a U.S. Air Force contract for the C100. General (Ret.) Tony Thomas, former SOCOM Commander, serves as Board Chairman.

Connected to the Zero Hour Group universe: PDW placed a $3.75 million order with Unusual Machines for NDAA-compliant components, and is an investor in Ondas Inc. ($ONDS).

Key products: C100 Tactical UAS, CORE mission software (v1.4 with GPS-denied navigation), Range Extension Kit, simulation training environments.
